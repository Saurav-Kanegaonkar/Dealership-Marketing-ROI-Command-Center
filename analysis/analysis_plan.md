# Analysis Plan

## Objective

Build a weekly operating artifact for a small multi-rooftop dealership group that needs to improve marketing ROI, service retention, data trust, and practical AI adoption.

## Steps

1. Generate synthetic dealership data at customer, campaign, source, and AI use-case grains.
2. Score service customers by recall status, state inspection timing, service lapse, declined work, Hunter alignment findings, response history, and repair order value.
3. Summarize paid search, paid social, email, recall outreach, declined service recovery, commercial prospecting, and third-party vendor spend by attributed gross and ROAS.
4. Flag source quality risks where freshness, match rate, or unclear ownership would weaken manager trust.
5. Produce dashboard-ready outputs for the ROI cockpit, retention queue, and data plus AI lab.
